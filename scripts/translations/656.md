This solution worked on my Droid Razr XT910, but I think it applies to other Android devices that have touchscreen problems while connected to USB or charging the battery.

Here's the tip:

Turn off USB DEBUGGING in the device's developer options.

The interesting thing is that I had already seen this problem on another device with the original cable and original charger. My RAZR also has original cables and chargers.

It fixed it for me — did it work for you?
