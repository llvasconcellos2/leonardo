A quick tip:

After installing CyanogenMod 11 (CM11) with Android KitKat, I noticed I couldn't find the Developer options in the Android settings. A quick Google search revealed the trick.

The developer options are hidden, and to unlock them you have to go to **Settings** – **About phone** and tap 7 times on **Build number**. Done — the developer options will now be available in the device's settings.

Another thing I noticed was different: when I tried to use adb shell I got the message **error: device offline**. I then realized the device was showing a message asking for permission to connect via adb. I granted permission and everything was fine. It even went straight into root.

That’s all folks!
